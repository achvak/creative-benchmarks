Benchmarks for casual creatives

The benchmarks for casual creatives, which includes puzzle, word, card, and coloring games, are on the low end of the spectrum. Metagames and a strong visual language make casual games more complex to wrap in a playable than other genres.

The average time-to-engage rate* for casual games is quicker than match-3 but longer than hyper-casual.

4.2 seconds avg.

Because the genre is so varied, tutorials for casual creatives aren’t that intuitive and require more thinking from the user, which is why engagement rate* is the most important metric for casual creatives to optimize.

In casual creatives, it’s slightly more difficult for the user to understand what’s needed from them at first, keeping engagement rates low.

The time-to-complete rate* for casual creatives is slightly longer than hyper-casual because they’re more difficult to complete - like solving a puzzle or finding a missing word.

17 seconds avg.

7.4 seconds avg.
