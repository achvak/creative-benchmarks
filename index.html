Benchmarks for hyper-casual creatives

Creatives for hyper-casual games have some of the highest in-ad data metrics in the industry. The genre lends itself quite well to the playable medium - they’re snackable, easy to understand, and surprisingly fun to lose.

The time-to-engage rate* for hyper-casual creatives that run on interstitial traffic is significantly shorter than on rewarded video traffic.

That’s because giving users the choice to opt-in forces users to think. And basic UX dictates that more choice is more complex.

Hyper-casual creatives have the highest engagement rate* out of all the genres.

Why? They function as mini-tutorials, are generally self-explanatory and don’t require much skill. There’s also very few elements to interact with, and it’s generally easier to simplify an already simple game into a tutorial.

The time-to-complete rate* for hyper-casual creatives is much shorter than other genres. And like the time-to-engage rate, is shorter for interstitial than rewarded video.
